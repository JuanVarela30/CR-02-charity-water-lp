About Us

At charity: water, we believe everyone deserves clean, safe drinking water — and you deserve to see the change you make possible.

Since 2006, we’ve funded over 111,000 projects, bringing clean water to more than 15 million people. Every donation is tracked and every story is shared, so you can see lives transformed by your generosity.

Clean water means healthier families, kids in school, and empowered communities. Together, we can create lasting change — one drop at a time.
